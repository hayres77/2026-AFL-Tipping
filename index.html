How It Works
Scoring rules · 2026 season
2 pts
Correct winner
Pick the winning team and you get 2 points — the base score for every correct tip, regardless of margin.
+1 bonus
Close margin
Your tipped margin is within 12 points of the actual winning margin. Stacks with the winner points for 3 total.
+2 bonus
Exact margin
Your margin lands within ±3 points of the actual result. Stacks on top of all other points — up to 8 pts per game.
+3 bonus
Upset pick
You correctly tipped the underdog — fewer than 40% of players picked that team. Stacks with all other bonuses.
×2
Double points rounds
Rounds 12 & 20 — every point doubles including bonuses. Announced the Monday of that week.
avg
Missed round
Didn't tip? You're auto-scored at the competition average for that round. Still a penalty versus actually tipping.

Round winner
Highest score each round wins $5. If two or more players tie, the pot rolls over and grows until someone wins outright.
